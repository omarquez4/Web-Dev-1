3 Things To Do During Autumn

As summer came to an end, I have the top 3 things you can do in the city of Chicago during Fall season! Check them out down below! To learn more about this activities make sure to click on the links provided

Apple Picking Pumpking Patch CornMaze

Apple Picking

We can for sure can get apples from the grocery store anytime during the year. However, there's something magical from going to pick them from the tree during harvest season. That crunchy, tangy, sweet apple flavor is at its peak during this time of year. It is a fun activity to go on your own or with your family. Make sure to checkout the places to go apple picking on the link above!

Pumpking Patch

Similar to apple picking don't buy your pumpkings from the grocery store. Instead go have some fun at a pumpking patch with your family. Check the link Pumpking Patch above for more info.

Corn Maze

Challenge yourself this fall season by figuring out the maze,and get some fresh air in the process. Corn mazes are fun for all ages.
